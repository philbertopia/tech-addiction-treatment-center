Our Addiction Treatment Programs

TATC (Tech Addiction Treatment Center) offers all the care and support you need to succeed with tech addiction.

Qunatum Partial Treatment

Our quantum care facility features state-of-the-art equipment and healing spaces that are specifically designed to enhance the therapeutic experience. We offer a range of treatments, including crystal meditation, energy healing, group energy sessions and subatomic healing chambers.

Intensive Outpatient VR Immersion Treatment

When clinically appropriate, IOP-VR is designed to allow individuals battling technology use disorder to attend therapy multiple times a week while still allowing them time to work and attend to other obligations.

Robotic Outpatient Companion Therapy

Robotic outpatient companion therapy is a type of therapy that utilizes robotic companions to provide quality, customized, and in-depth treatment programs offer the flexibility to maintain your career, family, and other important aspects of your life. These robotic companions are designed to mimic human-like behavior and interaction, providing individuals with a sense of companionship and comfort.

Nano Biotic Medication-Assisted Treatment

Trusted and reputable provider of Nano-bot Medication-Assisted Treatment (Nano-MAT) is a cutting-edge approach to addiction treatment that utilizes tiny robots, or nanobots, to deliver medication directly to the brain. These nanobots are designed to target specific areas of the brain that are responsible for addictive behaviors, providing precise and effective treatment.
